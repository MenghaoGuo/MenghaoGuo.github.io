Biography

    I am a final-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University (THU), where I have the great privilege of being advised by Prof. Shi-Min Hu. My current research focuses on foundation models, embodied AI, machine learning system, and 3D generation. Starting from June, I will be continuing my research as a postdoctoral fellow at the Department of Computer Science and Technology, Tsinghua University.
